Space Snapshot

Space Snapshot is a photography website whose main aim is to bring out the love for space exploration

It's main aim is to build the curiosity in a person such that they get to feel attracted in to the exploration world and even get to try it one fateful day.

There is a contrast between space and universe, The universe refers to everything physical in existence, from all celestial objects, matter, energy, galaxies, solar systems, etc. Whereas space refers to the empty void between all these things.
